Coping with Anxiety

  • Practice grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1)
  • Use journaling to externalize your thoughts
  • Try box breathing (inhale, hold, exhale, hold)
  • Know your triggers and manage them proactively

Dealing with Academic Stress

  • Use the Pomodoro technique for study breaks
  • Join a support group or find a study buddy
  • Speak with a lecturer, counselor, or mentor
  • Prioritize sleep, hydration, and nutrition
