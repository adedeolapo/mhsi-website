About Us

The Mental Health Support Initiative (MHSI) is a student-led movement committed to breaking the stigma surrounding mental health and equipping the university community with tools for wellness, resilience, and support.

Our Vision

To build a mentally resilient university community where students and staff feel empowered, supported, and equipped to prioritize their mental health without fear, stigma, or silence.

Our Mission

  1. Create safe and inclusive spaces for open conversations around mental health through awareness campaigns and peer-led support.
  2. Organize impactful programs like “My Mental Health and I”, with expert-led discussions, workshops, and wellness activities.
  3. Collaborate with professionals, university staff, and organizations to provide students access to practical coping tools and expert guidance.
  4. Empower students with resources and skills to manage academic stress, personal struggles, and life transitions constructively.
  5. Promote holistic education by integrating mental health advocacy into the broader campus culture.

Our Core Values

Empathy • Integrity • Courage • Connection • Community Impact
